Why Choose Us

College is Expensive. Choosing the right course during your limited college career is the best way to make your college experience worth its price. Carnegie Mellon University has a glittering variety of courses. They are like great products that sit silently on the rack of SIO, unattended. Evalution brushes the dust off of those courses and present them in the best way for students. This is what make a CMU experience special to every student at CMU.

Data Presentation

Instead of throwing ugly numbers in a table to students, Evalution present data in beautiful diagrams that can be understood at a glance.

Data Analysis

Evalution incorporates smart algorithms that analyze existing past year data from SmartEvals FCE and give students an intuitional understanding of the course they are comparing among.

Exposure

Evalution strive to add more meaningful questions for the Faculty and Course Evalutaion that will better help students to understand the course they are about to take.
